Hello and welcome to my portfolio. I am Ebram Abdelmasih, an architect from Egypt, graduated from the Faculty of Fine Arts, Minya University in 2021. I am currently pursuing my own learning journey in the field of architecture. I have a strong passion for architectural design and theory, and I believe in lifelong learning as a principle. Besides architecture, I also enjoy reading, music, theatre, photography, and video production as my hobbies.
