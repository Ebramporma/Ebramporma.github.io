we are shaped and fashond by what we love

One day I learned that some ophthalmology graduates learned in an intensive course about artistic criticism and how to analyse colors, shapes and lines, and how it differed with them in their work when they diagnose eyes better than their peers just by looking, as they trained their eyes to see what others do not see. I learned how to change a subject Study helps a person to look in a better way and to think in a creative way, so I decided to study everything I like at least to learn how things work with it. I thought about how music and theatre are related to architecture- things that I love and I love to participate in. I looked at music as rhythm, harmony, different layers, clear things, and other things in the background which I can see in Architecture as well, and how theatre is a distinct emotional state that the viewer feels when a person interacts with his feelings with different visual and audio effects which also I can use in architecture as well to make the user experience special, not just dealing With architecture as only a shelter for humans.

we are shaped and fashond by what we love.
